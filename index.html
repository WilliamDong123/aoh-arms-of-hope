Building a Caring Community for the Homeless

We are a student-led, non-profit organization dedicated to supporting homeless individuals with their unique needs, providing financial assistance, food, and essential necessities.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission

We strive to support the homeless according to their individual needs, providing financial assistance, food, and other essential necessities.

Our Vision

We are a student-led, non-profit organization, and our vision is to create a community where each homeless person feels supported by a caring community. We aim to help the homeless with their basic needs and support them to create a better tomorrow.

We Believe in Equality

Our core principle is equality. We believe every individual, regardless of their circumstances, deserves dignity, respect, and equal opportunities. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where everyone feels valued and supported.

Our Major Events

School Fundraiser

We will actively organize a fundraiser at school to provide financial assistance to homeless people. These events are crucial for gathering the resources needed to make a tangible difference.

Yearly Community Events

We'll also host yearly events to make our organization more fun and extend support to each individual, fostering a sense of community and belonging.

Direct Donation Trips

Additionally, we will arrange a donation trip where members/executives will deliver the money earned from events/fundraisers directly to homeless people.
